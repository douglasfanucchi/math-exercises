### Leibniz Notation

- $y = 5x^3 + 6x - 1$

- $s = \sqrt[5]{t} + \frac{3}{t}$

- $x =  \frac{t}{t + 1}$

- $y = tcos(t)$

- $y = \frac{u + 1}{ln(u)}$

- $x = t^3 e^t$

- $s = e^t tan(t)$

- $y = \frac{x^3 + 1}{sin(x)}$

- $y = \sqrt[3]{u}\cdot sec(u)$

- $x = \frac{3}{t} + \frac{2}{t^2}$

- $x = e^t cos(t)$

- $u = 5v^2  + \frac{3}{v^4}$

- $V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3$

- $E = \frac{1}{2} v^2$

- $E = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$, $m$ is constant

- $U = \frac{a}{x^12} - \frac{b}{x^6}$, $a$ and $b$ constants
